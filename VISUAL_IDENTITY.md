### Hero Section

- **Background:** Dark brand gradient.
- **Content:** Badge, Main Headline (H1), Sub-headline, Primary CTA, Secondary CTA.
- **Hero Statistics Bar:** Four statistics in a row, separated by thin vertical dividers, beneath CTAs.

### Information/Stat Cards

- **Layout:** Typically displayed in a 3-column grid (e.g., "The Crisis We're Solving" section).

- **Format:** Bullet points (`●`) for each event entry, including date, location, title, and description.
